OUR HERITAGE

Sulwhasoo began with a single insight: that beauty comes from harmony and balance. For over 50 years, we have been dedicated to unlocking the secrets of Asian medicinal herbs and traditional wisdom.

Our journey started with the pioneering research of Yun Dok-jeong, who dedicated her life to studying the power of ginseng and other precious ingredients. This passion became the foundation of Sulwhasoo, a brand that bridges ancient wisdom with modern science.

Today, Sulwhasoo continues to honor this legacy by creating products that restore balance to the skin and inspire holistic beauty that radiates from within.
